Protect Yourself

How you can protect youself:

There are many steps you can take to stay safe on the internet. Be sure to take precaution when clicking on or downloading from unknown or questionable sources. You can protect yourself against viruses with a few simple steps:

  • Run a more secure operating system such as Linux or Apple's Mac OS X.
  • Install a virus protection software as a safeguard.
  • Avoid programs from unknown sources on the internet and stick with commercial software purchased on CDs.
  • Make sure that Macro Virus Protection is enabled in all Microsoft applications. NEVER run macros in a document unless you know what they do.
  • Never click on an email attachment that contains an executable (files with an extension like EXE, COM or VBS).
