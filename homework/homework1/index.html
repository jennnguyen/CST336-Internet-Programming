What are Viruses

What are Viruses?

A computer virus type of malicious software program that is designed to spread from host to host and has the ability to replicate itself. Like how viruses in the human body cannot reproduce without a host cell, computer viruses cannot reproduce and spread without being programmed to do it such as in a file or document. Tens of thousands of computer viruses infect computers over the Internet, and every day, new computer viruses are discovered.

How are Viruses Spread?

Viruses can be spread through normal computer activities such as:

  • Sharing music, files or photos with other users
  • Visiting an infected website
  • Opening spam email or an email attachment
  • Downloading free games, toolbars, media players
  • Installing software without fully understanding the license agreements
